SUBPROGRAM: TRPAUS COMPUTE TROPOPAUSE DATA.

PRGRMMR: TREADON ORG: W/NP2 DATE: 92-12-22

ABSTRACT:
THIS ROUTINE COMPUTES TROPOPAUSE DATA. AT EACH MASS POINT A SURFACE UP SEARCH IS MADE FOR THE FIRST OCCURRENCE OF A THREE LAYER MEAN LAPSE RATE LESS THAN OR EQUAL TO A CRITICAL LAPSE RATE. THIS CRITCAL LAPSE RATE IS 2DEG/KM. THIS IS IN ACCORD WITH THE WMO DEFINITION OF A TROPOPAUSE. A MAXIMUM TROPOPAUSE PRESSURE OF 500MB IS ENFORCED. ONC THE TROPOPAUSE IS LOCATED IN A COLUMN, PRESSURE, TEMPERATURE, U AND V WINDS, AND VERTICAL WIND SHEAR ARE COMPUTED.
